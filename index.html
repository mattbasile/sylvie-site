Hi! I’m Sylvie. I’m a freelance writer living in Hudson, NY. My writing has appeared in Condé Nast Traveler, Edible Magazine, Architectural Digest, FOUND, amongst others.

In the fall of 2023, I received a MFA in Creative Nonfiction Writing from Columbia University.

In a past life, I helped launch restaurants and pop-up shops (and ate a lot) at the food culture agency, Care of Chan. I also worked alongside journalists, game designers, and chefs at The New York Times.

’m available for work of all kinds— including copywriting, interview & transcription work, research, and other general musings — so email me!
